Led a **10-member team** building a 6-wheeled rocker-bogie autonomous rover for the **ISRO Robotics Challenge 2024** (Indian Space Research Organisation), as part of the Robotics and Machine Intelligence Club at NIT Trichy.

## System

- **Rocker-bogie chassis** for passive terrain adaptation over rocks and slopes.
- **ORB-SLAM terrain mapping** with an Intel RealSense D435i depth camera.
- **Kinodynamic path planning** respecting the rover's steering and velocity constraints.
- **Custom position/speed-control electronics** designed and built in-house.

Alongside the rover, I also built **TOTO**, an autonomous mobile robot combining ICP-based SLAM with an Iterated Kalman Filter fusing wheel-encoder and RPLiDAR measurements, achieving ±2% velocity-tracking error on uneven terrain with closed-loop speed control.
